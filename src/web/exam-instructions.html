Exam Instructions for Students

These are general exam instructions, follow them in all the exams of this course unless stated otherwise.

The teachers expect you to know and follow this instructions, so read them carefully.

Things You Will Need During the Exam

These are the things you will need for the exam:

  • Your DNI or National Identity Card (no DNI, no exam).
  • A blue pen (better bring some spare blue pens with you, but do not bring a pencil case).
  • A simple watch (if you want to keep track of the time).

Everything that is not needed is prohibited, for example here is a list of things you cannot have during an exam:

  • Mobile phones or computers.
  • Pencils or non-blue pens.
  • Ink erasers, sticky tape.
  • Food (you are allowed a bottle of water and tissues, though).
  • Sheets of paper.
  • Backpacks, shoulder bags, pencil cases, glasses cases...
  • Books or other written materials.

(If you have some special medical needs, just explain them to a teacher when entering the exam room).

Before the Exam

Check the course schedule carefully. Make sure you know the time and room for the exam.

The exam begins at the specified time, sharp. My advise is to arrive at least 10 minutes before the exam is due to start. If you arrive late, you will not be allowed into the exam.

Ensure that you use the washroom before arriving to the exam.

During the Exam

Wait outside the room until we call your name, then enter the room and leave your belongings (backpacks, mobile phones, coats...) at the front of the class (just under the blackboard, in plain sight) and wait there until you are assigned a seat.

Make sure your mobile phone is switched off. If your phone rings during the exam you will get a -1 in the overall score and your classmates will hate you forever.

Once you are assigned a seat, sit there and do not move.

Once you have entered the room, you will not be able to leave until about an hour later or you will be graded as "NP" (no presentado). Once you leave the room you will not be able to enter it again.

Do not turn over exam papers until told to do so.

If you have any question or need a new exam booklet, raise your hand and a teacher will come to you. Teachers will not give hints or answers, so please do not waste your time asking for them.

Keep your eyes on your paper. Copying will be severely punished.

Talking is not allowed at any time in the exam room.

No extra pages will be provided for rough work. Everything you write on the exam booklet will be evaluated. Draw a line through rough work or other notes not considered part of your answer.

Stop writing immediately when the teacher says it is the end of the exam. This is extremely important, teachers will not give you a second warning, they will simply (and silently) not collect your exam if they has seen you writing after the time is due, and you will be graded an overall "NP" (no presentado).

If you finish the exam early, just raise your hand and a teacher will collect your exam, then leave the room quickly and quietly. Do not forget to collect your belongings before you go.

Unless stated otherwise, midterm exams extend for 2 hours. Ordinary and extraordinary exams extend for 4 hours.

After the Exam

Leave the exam room quickly and quietly and do not wait near the exam room for your classmates, just leave the building, as you will be talking too loud, bothering students still in the exam and other classes nearby.

We will publish the exam and its solutions a few hours after the exam.

We will publish your grades (via Aula Global) a few working days after the exam.
